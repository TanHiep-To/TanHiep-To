Scaling recommender systems for AI-native search

How to balance personalization, ranking, and system reliability.

Feb 2026 Tags: Recsys · Ranking Reading time: 4 min

Recommenders in AI search must serve multiple intents: relevance, personalization, and latency. A reliable system combines offline evaluation with online experimentation.

Key practices

  • Define objective-aligned metrics (CTR, dwell time, diversity).
  • Build a feedback loop with retraining schedules.
  • Use a unified feature store to keep training/serving consistent.
  • Monitor drift and bias with weekly audits.
