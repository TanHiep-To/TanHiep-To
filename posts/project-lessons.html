Lessons learned from shipping AI projects

What I learned from building RAG systems, recommender pipelines, and ML products.

May 2026 Tags: Projects · Delivery · MLOps Reading time: 4 min

Each project shaped a mindset: evaluate early, ship reliably, and optimize for the user. Below are the most useful lessons I reuse across AI and backend systems.

What worked

  • Define success metrics before training (latency, relevance, accuracy).
  • Build reproducible data pipelines and track experiment lineage.
  • Use lightweight evaluation to catch regressions quickly.
  • Collaborate with product teams early to align on outcomes.

Looking forward

My goal is to keep bridging research and production—turning academic ideas into practical AI products that scale.
