Learning Journey

My learning journey in AI

From foundational coursework to research-driven GenAI systems.

May 2026 Tags: Learning · AI · MSc Reading time: 4 min

My academic path started with core CS foundations—algorithms, data structures, and systems. The turning point was my undergraduate research in Generative AI, which led to a focused MSc track in AI and Machine Learning at VNU-HCM.

Learning phases

  • Foundation: CS fundamentals, software engineering, and math for ML.
  • Specialization: deep learning, computer vision, and NLP research.
  • Applied research: RAG, LLM fine-tuning, and evaluation pipelines.
  • Production: bridging research with reliable AI services.

What helped most

Project-based learning, mentorship from research labs, and shipping real systems at work. Each internship helped me understand how to balance model quality with product impact.
