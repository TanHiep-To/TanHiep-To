Trustworthy AI for cybersecurity detection pipelines

Robustness, calibration, and explainability for security ML.

Mar 2026 Tags: Security · XAI Reading time: 4 min

Cybersecurity models must be both accurate and trustworthy. This overview highlights the core practices for building interpretable and robust detection pipelines.

What matters most

  • Calibration: confidence scores that match reality.
  • Robustness: adversarial stress tests and drift monitoring.
  • Explainability: human-readable attributions for analysts.
