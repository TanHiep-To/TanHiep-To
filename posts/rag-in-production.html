RAG in Production: evaluation, grounding, and retrieval tuning

A field-tested checklist for reliable retrieval-augmented generation systems.

May 2026 Tags: RAG · Evaluation · Retrieval Reading time: 5 min

Production RAG systems live or die by retrieval quality. The model is only as good as the evidence you supply, so the evaluation loop must include retrieval, reranking, and grounding.

Core checklist

  • Define task-specific relevance metrics (precision@k, MRR) for retrievers.
  • Introduce a reranker to improve top-k evidence quality.
  • Track grounding rates: how often responses cite actual sources.
  • Monitor hallucination rate with a lightweight audit set.

Operational tips

Keep embedding and retrieval services versioned. Deploy retrieval changes behind feature flags and run offline evaluation before any online experiment.
